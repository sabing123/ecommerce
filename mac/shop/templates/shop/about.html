My Awesome Cart!

My Awesome Cart is an online marketplace and logistics company which operates in markets of South Asia and Southeast Asia. It was founded in 2020 as an online fashion e-commerce marketplace in Nepal by Muneeb Maayr Founder) & Farees Shah (Co-Founder) at the based venture builder, Rocket Internet. My Awesome Cart Group operates e-commerce platforms and logistics service in Bangladesh, Myanmar, Nepal, Pakistan and Sri Lanka. Sabin Gautam is the current CEO of My Awesome Cart.
